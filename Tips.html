Areas to visit

  1. Amboseli National Park – Famous for large elephant herds and stunning views of Mt. Kilimanjaro.
  2. Samburu National Reserve – Home to rare wildlife like Grevy’s zebra and reticulated giraffes.
  3. Tsavo National Park (East & West) – Kenya’s largest park, great for a rugged safari adventure.
  4. Lake Nakuru National Park – Famous for flamingos and rhino conservation.
